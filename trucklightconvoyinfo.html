Annual Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive information

The IEOA Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive is on Saturday December 3, 2022

Food Donations

In honor of the hard work the convoy participants have invested to create this festive event please consider supporting one of these local food banks with a donation. We are collecting food along the route or you can donate online anytime. Please add "Truck Convoy" in the dedication to your donation to refer to the event.
Please click on the charity of your choice, every $5 helps!



Food Donation Locations

Island Savings branches are open during business hours to receive your food and cash donations in support of our food drive with their year round community program, Full Cupboard.

  • Douglas and Broughton: 933 Douglas St, Victoria
  • Jubilee: 1955 Fort Street, Victoria
  • Brentwood: 1-7103 West Saanich Rd, Brentwood
  • Westshore: 2917 Jacklin Rd, Victoria
  • Mayfair:3195 Douglas Street, Victoria
